Jasmine Tookes is of African, Central American and British descent. She was born in February 1, 1991 and is currently 27. Born and rasied in Huntington Beach, California, she did gymnastics for 10 years, and took part in volleyball and softball. Inspired by Tyra Banks, she then became a model at age 15.
